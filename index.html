    About Us

    Welcome to our multi-purpose sports hub! Our versatile turf area, designed for both futsal and box cricket, offers endless possibilities for sports enthusiasts like you.

    Turf Area

    Experience the thrill of futsal on our 140 X 60 feet artificial turf. With a natural grass-like feel and weather-resistant properties, you can enjoy fast-paced matches all year round.

    • But that's not all! The same area transforms into a cricket haven with the introduction of box cricket.
    • Embrace the togetherness of this urban format, perfect for quick, action-packed games with your friends or colleagues.
    • The flexibility of our multi-purpose turf ensures that you can switch between futsal and box cricket seamlessly.

    Join us now and discover the joy of playing both futsal and box cricket in one convenient location. With our top-notch facilities and welcoming community, you're in for a sports experience like no other. Get ready to kick, bat, and bond with your fellow players on our dynamic sports turf!

    Services

    Our services are designed to cater to sports enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels, ensuring an exhilarating and inclusive environment for everyone.

    Futsal

    Experience the fast-paced excitement of futsal on our state-of-the-art turf. Perfect your dribbling, passing, and shooting skills as you engage in thrilling matches with friends or join our futsal leagues for a competitive edge.

    Box Cricket

    Unleash your cricketing passion with our action-packed box cricket arena. Gather your team and enjoy the urban format of this fast and fun version of cricket, guaranteed to keep the adrenaline pumping.

    Practice Nets

    Sharpen your cricket skills in our cutting-edge practice nets. Whether you're a seasoned batsman or a budding bowler, our three dedicated nets provide the perfect space to refine your techniques and elevate your game.
